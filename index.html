Aesthetic Anime Poet

A desktop automation tool that generates short-form poetry videos using AI narration, subtitles, and anime visuals, and uploads them to the creator’s own TikTok account for review or posting.

Terms of Service

This application is a desktop automation tool used by the account owner to upload videos they generate locally to their own TikTok account.

The app does not allow posting to third-party accounts. All uploads require explicit user authorization via TikTok OAuth. Users may revoke access at any time from their TikTok account settings.

Privacy Policy

This application uses TikTok OAuth to obtain permission to upload videos to the user’s own TikTok account. Only basic account information and content posting permissions required for uploading videos are accessed.

OAuth access tokens are stored locally on the user’s device and are not shared with third parties. No personal data is sold or used for advertising.
